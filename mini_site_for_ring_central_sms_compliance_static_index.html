Privacy Policy

We collect and process only the information needed to provide customer support and logistics services. This includes names, company details, phone numbers, emails, and communications metadata (time, date, and technical identifiers).

How we use data

  • To provide and improve support and fleet/monitoring services
  • To send transactional notifications related to your service
  • To comply with legal requests and prevent fraud/abuse

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing is limited to necessary service providers (e.g., telephony, email, IT), bound by confidentiality agreements, and to authorities when legally required.

Retention

Business records (including communications metadata) are retained only as long as necessary for operations and legal compliance. See the Call Recording Notice for recording‑specific retention.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, see Contact.

International transfers

Where data is processed outside your country, we rely on appropriate safeguards, including contracts with service providers.

Effective date: October 14, 2025

Call Recording Notice

We use our business phone system to record certain inbound and outbound calls for quality assurance, training, and dispute resolution.

Legal basis & consent

  • Where required, we obtain consent through audible notices at the start of the call (e.g., “This call may be recorded for quality and training purposes”). Continuing the call constitutes consent.
  • Upon request, our agents can continue without recording where feasible, or switch to alternative channels (e.g., email).

Scope

  • Content of calls, phone numbers, time/date, duration, and technical metadata may be processed.
  • Voicemails may also be stored and transcribed to improve response times.

Retention of recordings

Recordings are retained for up to 12 months unless a longer period is required due to an active investigation, dispute, or legal obligation. After the retention period, recordings are securely deleted.

Access & security

  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel with a business need.
  • We use industry‑standard controls (encryption in transit, access logs, role‑based permissions).

If you have concerns about recording, please inform the agent at the start of the call.
